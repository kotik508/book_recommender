Welcome to Bookie!

Bookie is here to help you discover new books that might interest you. Answer questions to get recommendations on the right-side panel. You can select up to 5 titles from the recommendations. When you have selected 5 books you can click End to finish. Otherwise the application will end automatically after 10 rounds of questions and display the best books for you. You can use the session code, which appears after starting a session, to return to that session anytime.

    Your task is to complete two sessions with these instructions:
  • find books based on your current preferences
  • find perfect books for your summer holiday

This project is made as a part of my Bachelor thesis and the data from here will be anonymized and used in it. By completing this study, you consent to the use of your data. If you change your mind, you can contact me about deleting this data at kotik.adam74@gmail.com.
