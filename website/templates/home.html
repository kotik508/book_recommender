Welcome to Bookie!

Bookie is here to help you find new books that might interest you. Answer questions to get recommendations on the right side panel. You can select up to 5 titles from the recommendations. When you have selected 5 books you can click End to finish, otherwise the application will end automatically after 10 rounds of questions and display the best books for you.

This project is made as a part of my Bachelor thesis and the data from here will be anaymized and used in it. There are two main approaches to Bookie. One is based on book descriptions and the second on book tags as indicated by the start button names. I would greatly appreciate it if you completed both versions as it is essential for the thesis.

Start by clicking either of these buttons.
