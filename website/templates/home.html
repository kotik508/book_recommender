Welcome to Bookie!

Bookie is here to help you discover new books that might interest you. Answer questions to get recommendations on the right-side panel. You can select up to 5 titles from the recommendations. When you have selected 5 books you can click End to finish. Otherwise the application will end automatically after 10 rounds of questions and display the best books for you.

You can use the session code, which appears after starting a session, to return to that session anytime. Try to complete two sessions, first trying to find a book based on your preferences. For the second session try to find a perfect book for your summer holiday!

This project is made as a part of my Bachelor thesis and the data from here will be anonymized and used in it.
